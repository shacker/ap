About athlete.photo

Three groups make up every marathon, century, track meet, mudder or triathlon: Participants, Organizers, and Photographers. athlete.photo is where those three groups come together.

Event Participants finally have a place not just to find the photos of them at their last big event, but from all of their events. Participants can tag themselves in photos and give them captions, even if they don't purchase the photos. Tagged photos are then grouped by event under your profile, creating a free gallery for all of your events.

Event Organizers have an easy way to publish details about an upcoming event, with maps, start times, notes, support and contact information, and a link to the main event site.

Event Photographers can register themselves as available in a given region, photograph participants during the event, and can publish photos here on athlete.photo. Photographers can choose their own watermarks, set their own prices, and show off their best photos.

Nutshell version: athlete.photo is where participants, organizers, and photographers come together on professional event and photograph-publishing platform.

How is athlete.photo different from other event photo sites? Rather than just purchase a photo and move on, here on athlete.photo, purchasing a photo allows other visitors to see the "unlocked" photo on your profile and in Event Galleries. Purchasing a photo unlocks it for everyone! Also, AP lets users upload their own photos, and people can tag themselves whether the main photog or another person uploaded them.
